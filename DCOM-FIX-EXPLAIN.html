DCOM FIX
The Latest updates to Windows  KB4056892 has broken the DCOM portion of Windows that allows for calls to programs/Drivers that use DCOM.
If your system is working OK, Please leave this page, and do not apply this fix.
Mr. Bob Denny of DC3 Dreams and a major contributor to ASCOM group has come up with a work around to fix the problem.  Thank you very much Mr Denny.
To see his testing of the problem please see his tests posted on the http://forums.dc3.com forum or the page repost of the problem on this site.
To see his work-around to fix the problem see his work-around posted on the http://dc3.com forum or the page repost of the fix on this site.
