Interactive Hand-Controller Menus - click on green button to Navigate
This is the MAIN GOTO Menu.

You can go to the list of catalogs stored in the hand controller,

The Solar System selection screen,

The coordinate Input screen,

The Bookmark screen,

and the Park mount screen from this menu.
