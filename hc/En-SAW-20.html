Interactive Hand-Controller Menus - click on green button to Navigate
You must hit "ALIGN" if this STAR is acceptable to add it to the model. 

If you do not hit "ALIGN" this star will NOT be added to the model.

Hitting Align will also take you to the menu that shows the alignment errors for your mount.

The reset is to clear the whole model.

Since this is the last star we are going to add for this demo, You have the choice of the following options.

Hit Align to be taken to see the model parameters.

Hit PAC to be taken to go to the PAC menu.

Hit BACK to go back to the main menu, to continue the hand controller investigation.
