Interactive Hand-Controller Menus - click on green button to Navigate
This is where you enter Latitude. Latitude is positive for the northern hemisphere and negative for the southern hemisphere. You enter degrees:minutes:seconds just like you did for the Longitude. This entry screen will take seconds, but the seconds will not be shown on the hand controller main site display.  To enter seconds you entry would look something like this: 33:26:15 if entering 15 seconds.

After you enter latitude hit "ENT", you will be taken back to the site menu.
