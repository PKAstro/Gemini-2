Interactive Hand-Controller Menus - click on green button to Navigate
The name of the stars are selected alphabetically.  We are going to enter a V to see if we can go to Vega. Hold down the UVW key till a V appears.  You can enter as many letters as you want. Hit Ent when done.


This screen has no button to get to the previous screen.  To get back, hit Ent, and then on the next screen use the Back button, which will take you back to the main GoTo screen.

The Del key deletes entered letters/numbers.
