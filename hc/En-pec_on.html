Interactive Hand-Controller Menus - click on green button to Navigate

This menu will appear if you have a PEC curve saved, and PEC is turned ON.

You can retrain PEC or turn PEC off.

Notice that the turn PEC On and turn PEC off button alternates with each other.

Also you can now select to have PEC start with tracking if you check the checkbox.  Of course PEC will have to be turned on the first time.
