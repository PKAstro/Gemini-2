Interactive Hand-Controller Menus - click on green button to Navigate
The mount is now slewing to the target.
When the slewing is finished you will receive a message that
GOTO DONE: Center Object.

Hit any of the 4 directional buttons to be taken to the next screen. This action is not what will happen with the mount, I only provided it to allow for you to get to the next screen sooner.
On Firmware hand controller firmware dated July 7 or later the Goto button has been renamed Menu.
