INDI Library for Gemini

INDI Library (https://www.indilib.org) is a cross-platform, cross-operating system library for operating various astronomical devices. INDI support Gemini and Gemini-2 devices and allows them to be used from other software.

In a way, the purpose of INDI is very similar to that of ASCOM platform and ASCOM drivers designed for Windows, but INDI is designed to also run on other devices, such as Raspberry Pi, Apple Mac, etc.

Please note that INDI and ASCOM standards are currently not compatible, so one can't be just replaced with the other, unless your astro software supports both.
